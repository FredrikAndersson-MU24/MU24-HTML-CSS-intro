Introduction

Welcome to this short text where I share my thoughts on learning in general and HTML in particular.

Getting started

There are several things to consider when preparing to learn a new programming language. At least for me. Below I have listed some of the most important ones.

  • Make a schedule - Schedule time for focused work and time for recovery
  • Focus - Turn off all notifications during your focus time
  • Take breaks - Get out of your chair and take a walk

More important stuff

Here are some more things that are easy to forget when you have got your study mode on, but it is imparative that you don't. Further down I give a more in-depth description of each topic.

  • Eat
  • Sleep
  • Love

Eat

It is crucial that your energy deposits are not depleted. Maintain a steady energy level, otherwise you might find yourself struggling trying to maintain focus.

Sleep

I have always heard that when you sleep is when you actually learn the things you've studied during the day. Having to little sleep can also affect your ability to maintain your focus during lessons.

Love

Hey, it is important to remember that there are other things in life than just work. Spend time with your loved ones as often as you can.
